Who We Are

Our Company

CareODrive is an All-in-One Social app that brings an end to the common pain that motorists in every big city goes through when they circle the blocks looking for a parking spot. Using break-through technology and Analytics, CareODrive provides all necessary information in relation to parking in real time and allows motorist to get parking slots in advance. It also provides Valet services on Demand and gives the freedom to go anywhere and do anything without looking or dealing with any problem related to parking.

Team Motive

Our main motive is to solve the huge crisis that the current society has related to parking by organizing it in a much better fashion than it already is, to process, calculate and provide analytical solutions and spots for your vehicle. We strive day and night to serve you better and are trying to change the way the world looks at parking by making it as simple as possible, within a touch.
